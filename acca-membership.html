Why It Matters

What your membership actually does.

ACCA is lean by design. Every dollar goes toward building the consumer infrastructure that was missing when SB 1548 nearly passed.

⚡

Rapid Response

When a bill moves, you'll know before the press does. ACCA monitors legislative calendars in Oregon, Hawai'i, and nationally so our network can mobilize before a hearing — not after.

Available to all members

📚

Research You Can Actually Use

We translate peer-reviewed science and government data into plain language arguments you can use with friends, family, and elected officials. No jargon. No industry spin. Just the facts.

Available to all members

🍻

Consumer Events

We're building a community of informed consumers across Oregon and Hawai'i through in-person happy hours, organizing meetups, and advocacy trainings. Sustaining members get first access and invites.

Supporter & Advocate tiers

🏛️

A Seat at the Table

Consumer voices change outcomes in hearing rooms. ACCA helped defeat Oregon SB 1548 with organized, evidence-based testimony. Your membership helps us show up every session — not just the ones that make headlines.

Advocate tier — named in testimony

Sustain the Alliance

Your support keeps the consumer voice in the room.

ACCA runs lean and volunteer-driven. Sustaining contributions fund event costs, research tools, rapid response infrastructure, and the operational basics that keep the org moving between sessions.

How funds are used

  • Consumer education events & happy hours
  • Research tools & legislative tracking
  • Website & rapid response infrastructure
  • Testimony preparation & hearing attendance
  • Grassroots organizing in Oregon & Hawai'i

Make a Contribution
